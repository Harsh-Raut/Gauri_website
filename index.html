The Artist

Meet Gauri Tidke

I'm a small-town girl who chose an acoustic guitar over science textbooks, eventually winning a televised reality show with my band. When the lockdown suddenly hit and everything collapsed, I found myself stuck in a comfort zone — until I realised music isn't just a performance, it's our rawest human connection.

I stepped out as an independent Hindi indie singer-songwriter, creating original songs that speak the things we feel but rarely say out loud. Music is the ultimate soundtrack to our loneliest tears and happiest celebrations. I record everything in single, honest takes to make you feel that undeniable power — and I can't wait to make you dance to my tunes.
